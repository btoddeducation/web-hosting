Blue Whales

Blue whales are crazy big and they live in the ocean. The largest-ever blue whale was measured to be 98 feet long.

Physical Characteristics

Blue whales are known for their immense size and unique blue-gray color.

Habitat

They can be found in various oceans around the world, preferring colder waters.

Dolphins

Dolphins are intelligent marine mammals known for their playful behavior and communication skills.

Communication

Dolphins use a variety of vocalizations and body movements to communicate with each other.

Behavior

They are often seen leaping and riding the bow waves created by boats.
